Geography

Like Olympia, Seattle is located off the shores of Puget Sound. Seattle is approximately sixty miles northeast of Olympia. Seattle is a major urban area, surrounded by other major cities. The Cascade Range and Olympic Peninsula encompass the city.

Seattle

The city of Seattle was first settled on November 13, 1851. The name came from a Native American Leader named Seattle. Chief Seattle was the leader of the Suquamish and Duwamish tribes, as well as other Puget Sound tribes.
